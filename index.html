Why Experts Say Attic, Garage & Rim Joists Are Key

According to the U.S. Department of Energy, up to 40% of a home's energy loss occurs through the attic and roof. Add in the garage (especially if it's attached) and the rim joists in your basement, and you're looking at the three biggest energy leaks in any home.

"If you only insulate three areas in your home, make them the attic, garage, and rim joists. These are the primary pathways for conditioned air to escape and outside air to infiltrate. Sealing these with spray foam is hands-down the most effective way to lower your energy bills."

— James Whitfield, Certified Energy Auditor, RESNET
